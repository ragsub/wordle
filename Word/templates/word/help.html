How to play the game?
  1. Guess the target word in 6 guesses
  2. Type the word and press enter to submit the guess
  3. The response color codes the letters to direct you to the target word
  4. Orange indicates the letter is present in target word but not in the same location
  5. Green indicates the letter is present in target word in the same location
  6. Gray indicates letter is not present in target word
